WealthCity Overview

Let us give you a small glimpse of WealthCity

"Build, Invest, and Plan for Your Financial Future – Start Today!"

WealthCity is a financial education application for teens which combines gamification with real-world financial planning. The aplication lets users build and manage a virtual city with different roles like investment analyst, financial manager etc and making investment decisions like buying stocks, real estate, and more, while learning essential money management skills. As users progress through different levels, they gain knowledge on budgeting, saving, and investing, all while simulating real-life financial situations. Later on, if the user is above 18, they can seamlessly transition from the virtual world to real-world investing(which is an option), using the skills and savings they’ve accumulated in the game.

Key Features:

Virtual City Building & Investment: Users make decisions to grow a virtual city, learning about investments and risk management along the way. Career & College Planning: The app helps teens understand the costs of education and career paths, providing tools to save for their future. Social Challenges: Teens can compete with friends in investment challenges or work together to achieve financial goals. Personalized Financial Advice: Users receive tailored advice to improve their money management and investment strategies.

Parental Monitoring: Parents can track their teen’s progress and contribute to their savings goals.

WealthCity offers two options for users:

Game Coins: Users can earn virtual coins through gameplay, which can be used to unlock premium content, challenges, and features. Real Money (Option): If the user is 18 or above, they can opt to convert their virtual coins into real money and start investing in actual financial platforms. WealthCity provides a fun and educational way for teens to learn about money, investments, and planning for the future.
