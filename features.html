Investment Simulations

WealthCity allows you to simulate a variety of investments in real-time, from stocks to mutual funds to real estate. You can test different strategies to grow your wealth without any risk involved.

Example: Imagine you want to invest in stocks. WealthCity allows you to simulate the market by choosing a company to invest in, track its growth over time, and adjust your portfolio strategy accordingly.
