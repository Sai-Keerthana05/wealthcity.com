User Journey

Learn how Wealthcity helps you achieve your financial goals step by step.

Step 1: Set Your Financial Goals

Start by defining what you want to achieve with your money. Whether it's buying a house, going to college, or retirement planning, setting a clear goal is the first step to financial success.

Example: If your goal is to buy a house, WealthCity will ask you for a target amount and date. It will then show you how much you need to save each month and track your progress towards your goal.

Step 2: Choose Your Investment Path

WealthCity will recommend different investment strategies based on your financial goals. These strategies include stock investments, mutual funds, savings accounts, and even real estate options.

Example: If you’re saving for a house, you might choose to invest in a conservative portfolio with bonds and savings accounts. If you’re saving for retirement, you might choose more aggressive stock investments.

Step 3: Monitor and Optimize Your Investments

Once you’ve started investing, WealthCity helps you monitor your progress in real-time. It also provides tips and recommendations to optimize your portfolio for the best return.

Example: If your stocks aren’t performing well, WealthCity will suggest adjustments, such as reallocating your investments into better-performing assets.
